ElGamal Encryption

SecureVote utilizes Exponential ElGamal to provide additive homomorphic properties necessary for the ZK-Tally.

# Key Generation

Private Key (sk) ∈ Z_q

Public Key (pk) = sk × G

# Encryption of Vote (v)

m = g^v (Exponential encoding)

C1 = g^k mod p

C2 = m × pk^k mod p

Ciphertext = (C1, C2)

RSA Blind Signatures

Used during the registration phase to authenticate a voter without the registrar knowing which ballot belongs to them, ensuring initial anonymity.
